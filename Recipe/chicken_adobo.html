Chicken Adobo

A classic Filipino dish sure to bring unique flavors to the lunch or dinner table. It is easy to prep and put together and is sure to become a staple in your menu.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs, wings, or drumsticks. (I prefer thighs with skin on)
  • Cooking Oil
  • Garlic cloves (chopped)
  • Soy Sauce 1/3 cup
  • Vinegar 1 cup
  • Whole peppercorn
  • Bay Leaves
  • Potato (optional)
  • Carrots (optional)
  • Hard boiled eggs (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat up pan and put cooking oil
  2. Saute Garlic
  3. Place chicken on the pan and cook both sides for 2 minutes until properly seared
  4. Add 1 cup of Vinegar
  5. Add 1/3 cup of Soy Sauce
  6. Stir until mixed
  7. Add whole peppercorn
  8. Add Bay leaves (2-3 leaves)
  9. Cover and cook for 20 minutes
  10. (optional) Add diced potatos after 20 minutes of cooking
  11. (optional) Add hard boiled eggs and carrots
  12. keep stirring and flipping chicken pieces back and forth and until finished cooking
