Spam Fried Rice

Short on time and looking for something super easy to toss together? look no further. This spam fried rice has been one of my number one go-to for a quick lunch whenever I'm on the go. A classic dish from the Pacific Islands sure to top your list of favorite dishes.

Ingredients

  • Spam (1 can)
  • Day old rice
  • eggs (4-6)
  • Cooking Oil
  • butter
  • Garlic cloves (chopped)
  • Soy Sauce 1/3 cup
  • Mixed veggies
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Dice whole block of spam and cook on separate pan tossing often
  2. Saute Garlic on wok
  3. Add butter
  4. Add eggs and scramble
  5. Add day old rice and separate with a spatula
  6. Add soy sauce and mix thoroughly
  7. continue mixing until rice is fried
  8. add Cooked spam and frozen mixed veggies
  9. continue tossing or mixing until veggies are cooked
